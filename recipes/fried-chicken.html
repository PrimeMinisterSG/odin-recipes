Fried Chicken!!

This is Fried Chicken which you can eat

Welcome to my recipe website where I will explain all about where I first ate a particular food and why it means so much to me. I will go into way too much detail telling you about that before actually getting to the recipe.

When I was 13 my parents let me out of my bedroom for the first time and drove me to KFC where I was given a trough of fried chicken to roll in. I will always remember my dad telling me to get out of the chicken trough, it was the first thing he ever said to me.

  • 500g chicken thighs
  • 2 tsp each of garlic powder, kosher salt, pepper and oregano
  • 1 cup plain flour
  • 1/2 cup cornlflour
  • 6 cups oil for frying
  1. Roll the chicken in the spice mix
  2. Throw the chicken in a pot of boiling oil (test the oil temp with your fingers first)
  3. Pull out the chicken when it turns black
  4. Let rest for 23 hours
