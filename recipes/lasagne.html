I was just a wee baby when I fell into a vat of lasagne, changing me into a hideous pasta-covered freak. Now I cannot live without a daily dose of lasagne blah blah blah.

  • 500g beef mince
  • 250g fresh pasta sheets
  • 500g jar of pasta sauce
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • cheeeeeeese
  1. Cook the beef mince until the pink has just turned to golden brown
  2. Throw in a raw onion
  3. Put the pasta sheets in a tray, cover with beef and onion
  4. Top with cheese and cook in the oven for 5 hours at 180 degrees C
